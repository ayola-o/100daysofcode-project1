Stash links from around the web

​​In a world where countless content is being sent out everyday, it is easy to miss out on the important things due to one thing or the other. Webstash allows you to stash any link you find interesting so you can come back and consume it at your own pace!

Safe place for your links

Webstash is not your average web bookmark. We have built a platform that is poised to change the way you interact with links . The best part is you can access your own stash from any device you own regardless of the platform or simply use our web application. Our browser extension and mobile application will get you started in minutes !

Manage your stash list

The only thing more important than having a stash is keeping your stash organized. With Webstash, you can easily sort your links into several categories based on either our preset classifications or your custom classification. It is also super easy to access your stash and sort through read and unread links.

Share your stash with friends

So you have carefully picked your stash to include links of cute Kittens and adorable puppies, we have made it easy for you to take your entire cat stash and share it to one friend or all your friends across all popular social media platforms.
